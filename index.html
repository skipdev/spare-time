5 Tips To Become More Successful As A Uni Student

1. Podcasts and Videos

If you have nothing productive to do consider in using that time wisely by listening to Podcasts and Videos relative to your course. Not only is this a good way to help you learn but is a better method than reading articles or books.

2. Blogs

Blogs are a brilliant way of expressing your passion in the things you love doing. Not only to say that they are a great way of wasting time.

3. Putting Yourself Out There

Using social media can help enhance your chances of employability and find out news on the latest things that interest you. For example - Twitter is a great networking tool as it connects you between you and your other colleages in a team project. Having a LinkedIn account also helps you promote yourself to potenital new employers.

4. Procastinating

Procastinating is one big issue alot of us deal with everyday. However when you procrastinate you waste more time and makes it harder to achieve your goals.

5. Schedule your day

Scheduling your day the night before helps you to get things done the next day and makes you know what is needed to done.
